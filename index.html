The Training Strategy

A Push/Pull/Legs (PPL) split is efficient and effective. To maximize growth, each muscle group should be trained at least twice a week.

Split Type Frequency Suitability Example Schedule
3-Day PPL 1x/week per muscle Suboptimal (Maintenance) Mon: Push, Wed: Pull, Fri: Legs
4-Day PPL (Alternating) ~1.5-2x/week per muscle Recommended for Growth W1: P,P,L,P | W2: P,L,P,P
5-Day PPL (Rotating) ~1.4x/week (every 5 days) Excellent (High Flexibility Needed) P, P, L, Rest, P, P, Rest...

The V-Shape Workout Arsenal

This dumbbell-only routine focuses on the key muscles for a V-Taper: wide shoulders and a broad back.

PUSH DAY

Focus: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

  • Dumbbell Bench Press3x 10-12
  • Overhead DB Press3x 12-15
  • Dumbbell Flyes3x 10-12
  • DB Lateral Raises3x 15-20
  • Dumbbell Front Raises3x 12-15
  • Tricep Overhead Ext.3x 12-15

PULL DAY

Focus: Lats, Back, Biceps

  • Bent Over DB Row3-4x 8-12
  • Dumbbell Pullover3x 10-12
  • Reverse Flyes3x 10-15
  • Hammer Curls3x 10-15
  • Flip Grip Curls3x 10-12

LEG DAY

Focus: Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes

  • Dumbbell Goblet Squat4x 10-12
  • Dumbbell RDLs4x 8-10
  • Dumbbell Lunges3x 10-12
  • Bulgarian Split Squat3x 8-12
  • Standing Calf Raises3x 12-15

Progressive Overload is Key!

To keep growing, you must challenge your muscles. Gradually increase weight, reps, sets, or decrease rest time.
